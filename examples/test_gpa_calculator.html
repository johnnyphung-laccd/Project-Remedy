How to Calculate Your GPA

Figuring Out Your Grade Point Average

Formula: Total Grade Points ÷ Units Attempted = Grade Point Average (GPA)

Grade Point Values

  • A = 4 points
  • B = 3 points
  • C = 2 points
  • D = 1 point
  • F = 0 points

Example Calculation

Example GPA Calculation showing how to compute grade points
Classes Units × Grades Point Value = Grade Points
Business 120 4 × A 4 = 16
English 120 3 × B 3 = 9
History 114 3 × C 2 = 6
French 120 5 × A 4 = 20

Totals: 15 units attempted, 51 total grade points

Result: 51 total grade points ÷ 15 units attempted = 3.40 Grade Point Average
